Copy decisions applied (v2 sheet)

  • Tier → Package across all user-facing copy.
  • Grace limit → Plan + buffer. Buffer explained in toggle description.
  • Autocharge → Auto-upgrade. Old toggle copy was conceptually wrong (it wasn't about charging, it was about package upgrades).
  • Cancel subscription is grayed out per Jira spec. Click shows Account Manager modal.
  • Date format: Jun 12, 2025 everywhere (not 11/06/2021).

  • Warning icon next to periods with paused subs, with tooltip.
  • "Additional packages" column removed — booster info moved inline into the Package column chip (e.g. "2,000 + 1×500 booster").

Cancel RevRec subscription

ℹ️
RevRec can't be fully canceled from here. Because deferred revenue data is tied to your accounting books, cancellation requires a short sync-down process with your Account Manager.

Your Account Manager will:

  • Export your historical RevRec schedules
  • Confirm the last billing period
  • Downgrade your package to the smallest available tier, or schedule a clean close-out
