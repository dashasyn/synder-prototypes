# Empty State Feedback: Mike Torres (Business Owner)

**About me:** Shopify store owner, ~$180K/year revenue, zero accounting background. I handle my own books but I learn as I go. I'm busy and anything that sounds complicated or risky goes to the bottom of my to-do list.

---

## Why I Wouldn't Click "Start Reconciling"

**1. I don't know what "reconciliation" means**
Seriously. I see "Transaction reconciliation" and I think: *Is this something my accountant should do? Is this about taxes? What happens if I mess it up?* The word sounds official and scary, like I'm about to file something with the government.

**2. "Start reconciling" sounds irreversible**
When I see "Start," I assume something BIG is about to happen. Will it change my numbers? Will it mess up my books? Can I undo it? There's no indication that I'm just *setting up* something—I feel like I'm about to trigger a process I can't stop.

**3. "Beta" + financial data = NOPE**
Oh hell no. You're telling me this feature is still being tested and you want me to try it on my actual money? I'm not a guinea pig for my financial data. What if it deletes something? What if it creates duplicate transactions? I'll pass.

**4. Six FAQs? That's homework**
If I need to read six FAQs to understand what a button does, the button failed. I'm running a business, not studying for an exam. I see all that text and I close the tab.

---

## What Would Make Me Feel Safe

- **Show me it's just a setup form**, not an immediate action
- **Tell me nothing gets changed until I review it**
- **Remove "Beta" or at least explain what "Beta" actually means for my data**
- **Give me a preview or demo**—show me what this does before I commit

---

## Copy/UX Changes I'd Respond To

| Current | Better |
|---------|--------|
| "Start reconciling" | "Set up auto-matching" or "Connect your accounts" |
| "Transaction reconciliation" | "Match your Shopify payouts to your bank" |
| "Beta" badge | "New" or remove it entirely |
| 6 FAQs | One-liner: "This matches your bank deposits to Shopify payouts—nothing changes until you approve it" |

---

## My Ideal Empty State

1. **Plain language headline:** "See which Shopify payouts hit your bank"
2. **One sentence explaining the value:** "Stop manually hunting for deposits—see matched and unmatched transactions in one place."
3. **CTA:** "Connect your bank account" (not "Start")
4. **Reassurance line:** "Your books won't change. You review everything before it's recorded."
5. **Optional:** A 30-second video showing what it looks like when it's working

That's it. If it takes more than that to explain, it's too complicated for me right now.

---

**Bottom line:** I don't click because I'm scared I'll break something. Make it feel like browsing, not committing, and I'll try it.
