How should we sync your data?

Choose how transactions flow into your accounting software.

Recommended

Per transaction

Every sale, fee, and refund syncs as an individual entry in your books.

  • Individual transaction records
  • Tax tracking per state/country
  • Customer & invoice tracking
  • Product cost tracking
  • Inventory quantities
For high volume

Summary

Daily/weekly journal entries that summarize all transactions together.

  • Summarized journal entries
  • Auto-reconciliation
  • COGS & inventory assets
  • Tax & AR totals
  • Income by product/SKU
⚠️ Heads up: Changing sync mode later requires creating a new organization. Most businesses start with "Per transaction."
