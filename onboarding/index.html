How should we sync your data?

Choose how transactions flow into your accounting software.

Recommended

Per transaction

Every sale, fee, and refund syncs individually.

  • Individual transaction records
  • Tax tracking per state
  • Customer & invoice tracking
  • Product cost tracking
For high volume

Summary

Daily/weekly journal entries summarizing all transactions.

  • Summarized journal entries
  • Auto-reconciliation
  • COGS & inventory assets
  • Income by product/SKU
ℹ️Changing sync mode later requires a new organization. Most businesses start with "Per transaction."

Connect your books

Allow Synder to record and organize your transactions accurately.

What Synder will do:

  • ✓ Add transactions and create records in your books
  • ✓ Reconcile data to keep balances accurate
  • ✓ Ask for your approval before creating new accounts

Your control:

  • 🛡 You can undo synced data at any time
  • 🛡 Nothing is changed without your confirmation
🔍 Accounting first: The backend requires an active accounting connection before sales integrations can be linked. The two-column trust/control block reduces anxiety before the OAuth redirect.

Connect your payment platform

Let Synder import your transaction data for the selected period.

What Synder will do:

  • ✓ Securely import transactions for the period you choose
  • ✓ Use the data to prepare accurate records in your books

What Synder won't do:

  • ✗ No changes to your payment platform
  • ✗ No posting, editing, or deleting data
  • ✗ No invoices or records created in the platform
