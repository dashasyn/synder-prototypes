## Key Findings (Onboarding Prototype v4)
### 🔴 Universal (all 3 flagged)
1. Sync mode = conversion killer ("requires new org" terrifies users, 40-50% quit risk for non-accountants)
2. Jargon never explained (reconcile, journal entries, COGS)
3. Revenue field feels invasive — no explanation of why it's needed

### Accountant-specific
- No multi-client scalability info
- Missing Chart of Accounts mapping
- Landing page ignores accountant audience
- No cash vs accrual question

### Business owner-specific
- Sage Intacct/NetSuite make small sellers feel "wrong place"
- "Select all that apply" missing on platforms
- "Intuit" SSO — doesn't know that's QuickBooks

### Quick wins (Viktor)
- Add "~3 minutes" to welcome screen (5 min fix)
- Revenue tooltip (5 min fix)
- Jargon tooltips (1 hour)
