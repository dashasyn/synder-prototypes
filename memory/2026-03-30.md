## Unsubscribe Flow Audit (2026-03-30)

### What happened
- Ignat asked me to check the Synder unsubscribe flow
- He created an org called "Unsubscribe" specifically for me to test
- Traced the full current flow (6+ steps) via Playwright on demo.synderapp.com
- Pulled LogRocket data: 2,077 sessions on manageSubscription page in 90 days, only ~9% complete cancellation
- Reviewed 14 Figma frames from the new unsubscribe redesign (Figma file: FIzUFVfEC8dOCtq0gL0qhV)
- Key new addition: contextual retention screens per cancellation reason (discount for "too expensive", schedule-a-call for "didn't understand", etc.)
- Provided detailed feedback on copy issues, taxonomy problems, missing elements

### Key findings
- Current flow: 6+ clicks, inverted button hierarchy, contradictory data deletion messaging (7 days vs 1 year)
- LogRocket: "Too expensive" is top cancellation reason; 30% of manage-subscription visitors are just fixing failed payments (AmEx issue)
- New design strengths: radio buttons, progressive sub-reasons, per-reason retention screens
- New design gaps: "Schedule a call" missing for high-plan "too expensive" users, copy errors, taxonomy overlaps, no progress indicator
