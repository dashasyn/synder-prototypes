## Manage Subscription Prototypes — 3 Variants Built (1:41 PM)

Built 3 prototypes for Synder's Manage Subscription page after Ignat flagged the current page as chaotic (especially with RevRec coming).

**File:** `synder-prototypes/manage-subscription/index.html`
**Live URL:** https://dashasyn.github.io/synder-prototypes/manage-subscription/

**Three variants:**
1. **Tabbed Organization** — Overview / Add-ons / Usage / Billing tabs
2. **Accordion Layout** — Expandable add-on items, current plan always visible
3. **Dashboard Style** — Two-column with status banner + monthly total sidebar

**Persona feedback (Sarah CPA + Mike business owner):**

**Unanimous winner: Prototype 3 (Dashboard)**

**Why P3 wins:**
- Monthly total always visible in sidebar ($292.80 = $79 + $118.80 + $95) — P1 and P2 both miss this entirely
- Status banner shows next billing date + amount upfront
- Usage meters visible, not hidden behind tabs/accordions
- Quick Links surface "Cancel Subscription" — paradoxically makes users feel in control

**P1 killed by:** strikethrough $500.99 → $79 spikes anxiety ("am I being charged $500?"); 4 tabs = bill could hide anywhere
**P2 killed by:** everything collapsed by default; total buried at bottom; users have to click to see what they're paying for

**Sarah's blockers for P3 before ship:**
1. Status banner should turn yellow at 75% usage (currently green even when Smart Rules at 70%)
2. Add "Manage" affordance to add-on rows (currently look static)
3. Payment expiry warning chip when card expires within 30 days
4. Move "Cancel Subscription" out of Quick Links — same visual weight as "View Invoices" = misclick risk

**Mike's ask:** Add "?" tooltips for "RevRec" and "Smart Rules" with plain-English explanations. Non-accountants don't know what these mean — either won't buy or panic they're already paying for it.

**Sarah's fast-followers:** projected overage line, prorated charges, billing history preview in sidebar, Export CSV, clarify "1,000 syncs left" vs "500 of 10,000 transactions" mismatch, org selector search.
