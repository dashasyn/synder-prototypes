## Morning Idea — Accessibility/Edge Case
- **Topic:** Progressive enhancement for pricing yearly toggle
- **Angle:** Accessibility/edge case (from April 15 pricing prototype)
- **Idea:** Show both prices in HTML (monthly strikethrough + yearly bold), then use JS to collapse into toggle. No JS = users still see the 20% OFF deal. Helps screen readers, ad blockers, crawlers. Zero visual change for 99%, full information for the 1% with JS failures.
- **Built on:** April 15 pricing prototype — yearly toggle with "20% OFF" badge assumes JS works; edge case is invisible broken experience


## Pricing Prototype — 5 Variants Complete (4:00 PM)

Built 5 pricing page variants for Synder's Pro/Pro Max tier decision:

**Version 1:** 4 cards (Basic, Essential, Pro, Premium) — baseline
**Version 2:** Modal approach — "Compare Pro options" button opens modal with Standard vs Max side-by-side
**Version 3:** Inline toggle — single Pro card, toggle switches between Standard/Max views
**Version 4:** Wide Pro card — Basic, Essential, wide Pro (Standard+Max side-by-side), Premium
**Version 5:** 5 cards — all tiers as separate cards including Pro Max

**Key iterations from persona feedback:**
- Modal (V2) preferred over toggle (V3) — less decision paralysis
- "Compare Pro options" clearer than "Adjust tier"
- Price jump $275→$700 needs clearer value justification
- Transaction/sync terminology confusion flagged by multiple personas
