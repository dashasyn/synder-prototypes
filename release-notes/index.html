Important: Detaching and re-attaching Stripe payments is not supported
Stripe's manual detach/re-attach of payments is not supported by Synder and can cause sync issues
⚠ Limitation Stripe Per Transaction Summary Sync
Availability: Summary Sync, Per Transaction Accounting: QuickBooks Online, Xero, Oracle NetSuite, Sage Intacct, Puzzle Integrations: Stripe

What's new

Stripe introduced the ability to manually detach payments from invoices and re-attach them to other invoices directly in Stripe.

Why it matters

Manual detaching or re-attaching of payments in Stripe isn't currently supported by Synder and can lead to skipped records or incorrect Accounts Receivable balances after sync.

If you detach or re-attach payments in Stripe, Synder will not reflect those changes automatically. You'll need to manually review and adjust affected invoices and payments in your accounting system.
